- **Format**: A4 portrait (210 × 297 mm), print-at-home
- **Pages**: Cover + 12 monthly pages + notes area per month
- **Illustrations**: High-quality botanical art (1536×1024 px)
- **Calendar**: Full 2027 date grid with day headers, weekend highlighting
- **Year**: 2027
